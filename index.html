Our Story

About Us

GoLazy is the umbrella brand for LazyGames and ActiveGames. Our studio was founded in 2023 by developers active since 2017. With years of experience in game development, we bring creativity and quality to every project.

We're best known for our simulator games, but we're expanding into new genres with fresh ideas. From concept to launch, we focus on creating fun, high-quality experiences for players worldwide.
